Campaign Engine

Cold starting your project is made a breeze with Aki Network. Our campaign engine chooses the most suitable influencers and scales Web3 projects for higher ROI.

StarMap

StarMap is a user-friendly dashboard that uses our in-house benchmarking algorithm to quantify influencers' Web3 impact into scores with both Web3 and Web2 data.

Influencers may demonstrate their impact, locate opportunities, and engage their audience directly here. Projects can locate the right ambassadors based on real data insights.

Aki Profile & Aki InScore

Aki Profile is a permissionless personal Web3 landing page. It onboards our community data to StarMap and Aki Protocol. Aki InScore uses Aki Native Data from our network and campaigns to determine user influence.

Every Web3 user is welcome to take Aki Profile as a starting place to look for their favorite Web3 voices and ideas.

Aki Badges, soul-bound non-fungible tokens (SBT), reward users and influencers for participating in Aki Network marketing campaigns. Holders can collect cash stablecoins and/or token rewards at end of each season.

Our target is that every data contributor to Aki Protocol will get rewarded for growing our network and Aki Protocol.
